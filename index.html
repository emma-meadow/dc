This is one of the most important sections for our team.

Why this matters: A physician or coroner must sign the death certificate before it can be filed. Providing accurate doctor information is the single most important thing you can do to help us track down the right person quickly — which directly affects how fast we can complete and file the certificate on your behalf. Please be as complete as possible.
